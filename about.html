The Zabrosa Initiative

We are a passionate team of computer science enthusiasts working together on a groundbreaking project: Zabrosa City in the Making. Our goal is to apply advanced Design and Algorithm Analysis (DAA) techniques to develop sustainable, data-driven solutions for modern urban challenges, such as optimized resource allocation, traffic flow, and infrastructure planning.

Our goal is to integrate technology and innovation to create systems that are scalable, efficient, and user-friendly, demonstrating the practical application of theoretical computer science in real-world infrastructure.
